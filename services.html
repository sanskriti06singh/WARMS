Our Services

At WARMS, we are dedicated to empowering widowed women by providing a range of services tailored to their needs.

Community Support

Build connections with like-minded individuals and find strength in our supportive community.

Skill Development

Participate in workshops and training sessions to learn new skills and enhance employability.

Mental Health Support

Access professional counseling and mental health resources to navigate challenges effectively.

Financial Assistance

Get guidance and resources to achieve financial stability and independence.

Housing Assistance

Explore our programs to find safe and affordable housing options.

Legal Aid

Receive expert advice and assistance for legal matters related to widowed status.
