About WARMS

We believe in creating a world where every widow can find hope, strength, and the support she deserves.

Who We Are

WARMS (Widowed Assistance and Resource Management System) is a community-driven initiative aimed at empowering widowed women.

Our Mission

To uplift widowed women by offering comprehensive support services, skill-building programs, and financial independence tools.

Our Vision

To foster a society where widowed women can thrive without stigma, fear, or limitations.
